THINGS TO DO
Since you travelled all the way up here, you might as well enjoy your stay! Here's a few things we recommend you try:

Board Games at Small Print Board Game Cafe

Try some of our favorite coffee shops: Kettle Black or Receiver Coffee Co.

Brunch at Casa Mia Café or Leonhard's Café and Restaurant

Grab a bite to eat at The Gahan House, The Brickhouse, or Piatto Pizzeria

Go skating at one of the indoor or outdoor skating rinks in Charlottetown

Browse the art gallery at the Confederation Art Gallery

Go bowling at Murphy's Community Centre

Browse the local library: Confederation Public Library

Tour some historic buildings: Province House, Charlottetown City Hall, All Souls' Chapel, Beaconsfield Historic House, St. Dunstan's Basilica

Play some indoor golf at All About Golf

Watch a movie at City Cinema

Treat yourself to some delicious ice cream at Cow's

Do some winter sports at Brookvale Winter Activity Park
Note: located about 25 minutes outside of Charlottetown


In addition, our wedding day coincides with Canada’s 150th Anniversary celebration, so there will be a lot going on in town! See below for more information about the fesitivies: http://city.charlottetown.pe.ca/Canada150.php
